ü§ñ What is Social Robot Navigation?

üí° Definition:
"Robots navigating among humans while respecting social norms, ensuring safety, and operating efficiently."

‚úîÔ∏è Importance:
Social robot navigation is crucial for seamless integration into environments such as healthcare, hospitality, and public spaces. Advanced predictive decision-making is essential to manage inherent unpredictability.

Predictive Models

(RNN, LSTM, GRU, Transformers)

Introduction

  • üéØ Motivation: These models learn from sequential data (e.g., human motion, sensor readings), making them suitable for dynamic, real-world tasks.
  • üè∑Ô∏è Key Benefit: By predicting future states, robots can proactively plan and avoid collisions or uncomfortable interactions.
  • ü§ù Relevance: Critical in social navigation contexts where anticipating human behavior is essential.

Comparison of Predictive Models

Model Description üéØ Pros ‚ö†Ô∏è Cons
RNN Basic recurrent net passing hidden states across time üéØ Simple, good for short sequences ‚ö†Ô∏è Vanishing/exploding gradients for long sequences
LSTM Adds gating (input, forget, output) for long-term dependencies üéØ Mitigates vanishing gradients; better for lengthy sequences ‚ö†Ô∏è More parameters, heavier computation
GRU A simplified LSTM with fewer gates üéØ Fewer parameters, faster training than LSTM ‚ö†Ô∏è May capture slightly less nuance for very long sequences
Transformers Employ self-attention for global context üéØ Excels at capturing long-range dependencies ‚ö†Ô∏è High memory & compute requirements

Cosine-Gated LSTM (CGLSTM)

üìå Introduction

  • ‚úÖ Proposes a novel LSTM-based architecture with a cosine similarity gate.
  • ü§ñ Aims to improve sequence prediction by focusing on directional alignment.
  • üìå Builds on limitations observed in previous LSTM experiments (e.g., in SocNavGym).

üîç Fundamentals & Limitations

üí° Why Cosine-Gated LSTM?

  • ‚úÖ Cosine similarity emphasizes vector orientation over magnitude.
  • ü§ñ Helps the model focus on the most relevant sequence features.
  • üìå Leads to more robust gating and improved long-term dependency modeling.

Entropy‚ÄêDriven Adaptive Prediction Horizons

üìå Introduction
